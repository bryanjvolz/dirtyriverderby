The Plastic Cup is a Supporters tournament played by the Louisville Coopers and Cincinnati Pride before each installment of the DRD.

Reschedule

The final game of the 2018 DRD was split over two days, with the originally scheduled game being abandoned in the 38th minute due to rain. That game saw a 0-1 lead for Cincinati which was held over for the rescheduled date. Tuesday, September 11th was chosen and Cincinnati held on to the scoreline to win the match.
